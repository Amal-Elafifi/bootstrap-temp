How Can I Request A Refund?

I want you to be so excited about every item you purchase from me so if you don't love your garment, send it back! To begin processing your return please go to: returns.fashionbrandcompany.com We offer full refunds on all regular priced items but there is $5 restocking fee per garment. The restocking fee is waived if you opt to get your return in the form of store credit however. Once a return arrives on site it will take a few days for us to confirm that the item was returned undamaged and in good condition. We aim to process returns as quickly as possible, but it may take up to 10 days after it’s delivered back to us for your item to be refunded. Please do not

Can I Exchange For Another Size?

Yes! We are able to accommodate exchanges for items that are in stock. To begin processing an exchange please go to returns.fashionbrandcompany.com to begin your exchange or refund. Please keep in mind that we make our clothes in very limited batches and so we may not be able to accommodate an exchange in your size of choice.

Can You Help Me With Customs Fees?

We are not responsible for customs fees or holds by customs. You will be required to pay any additional fees to receive your package. If your package is not collected and sent back to us we will refund the order but not the shipping fees. We make an effort to be aware of accurately marking packages for customs to avoid our customers incurring excessive fees.

My Item Arrived Damaged From Shipping

We offer Route package protection automatically on all orders to protect against this exact issue. You can file a claim directly through Route to get a refund or a replacement. If you are a dingdong and opted out of this protection there is nothing we can do to help you with this issue.
